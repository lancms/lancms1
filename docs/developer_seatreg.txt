These are the "seattypes" (in SQL: [prefix]_seatReg.type):

d - open seat
p - password protected seat
g - group protected seat
t - text (for writing text in the seatmap)
b - blank/white/open space
w - wall
o - door/opening
a - area (kiosk-area, security-area, infomationdesk-area, gamecrew-area)
